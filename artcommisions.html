Art Commission Rules

Welcome to my art commission page! Here are the rules you need to follow:

  • All commissions are free for you to use as you see fit, but credit is appreciated if you post on IG or Threads. See the main page for my socials. Mentions on Discord should use @silent.
  • As far as I'm concerned, AI or human remakes of my art are okay, as it's now your art. But be sure to credit me as the original artist for visibility.
  • Payment must be made upfront, unless otherwise arranged.
  • Refunds are accepted up until the finished art is delivered.
  • Turnaround could be two to three days, depending on the complexity of the piece, my life, and my ADHD focus.
  • I'd like to change my art if it's not up to your standards, but please do be mindful of my time and not too demanding.

Any commissions will be subject to these rules, and will result in a ban on the appropriate platform for where you requested it if you don't follow them, and if I especially feel you are trying to spam (most won't, if the rules are followed), you will be reported to the FCC and platform.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out!
